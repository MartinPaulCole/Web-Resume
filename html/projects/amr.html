AMR Meters

Automatic meter reading (AMR) is the technology of automatically collecting consumption, diagnostic, and status data from water meter or energy metering devices (gas, electric) and transferring that data to a central database for billing, troubleshooting, and analyzing. (ref. Automatic meter reading)

The project I was involved with as a contratcor was to assist in the migration existing firmware which monitored pulse metering devices by Meridian Energy to new GE SM110 Metering devices The meters contained configured real-time data collected from measuring the power consumption of the connected supply. The main informtaion we used was KWh, however it was capable of measuring many other sources (e.g. water flow). In-house boards were fitted with RF Mesha and GSM capability to allow backhaul to the Meridian servers for billing purposes.
