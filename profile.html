PERSONAL PROFILE
I am an Embedded Software Developer with over 25 years of experience. A large amount of my experience comprises of work within the telecommunications industry. This includes Mobile Radio equipment for ten years and six years working on SCADA radio products.

I have also worked in the Consumer Products, Household Power Management, Automotive and, most recently, Fire Detection Systems.

I enjoy working in all stages of software development and have used many platforms (both large and small).

I have experience in C and assembly language equally. I have worked on a range of processors and Operating Systems. These processors include NXP, Renesas, Motorola HC11, Atmel, ST and TI DSPs.

I am comfortable working on driver layer software as well as the core level and application layers within the embedded domain.

The operating systems I have used range in size from proprietary NEC Exchange OS down to a small ECos, Micrium, MicroC-OS, Bare Metal as well as my own proprietary scheduler designs.
